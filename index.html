Click Buttons


Times

Click dropdown button and choose different periods to show ratings of movies for each year, or choose 1940 – 2000 for all periods.

This part is designed for audiences, who can pick movies according to year and rating.


Length/Budget

Show the changes of length/budget of movies from 1940 to 2005, with the line showing the average.

This part is designed for movie producers, who can observe the trends over 65 years.

Interaction


Hover mouse over the circles to see detailed information.

Click circles to mark and compare in different plots.




Color-coding


Circles are color-coded by genre.

Afterword


The reason why I chose the movie topic is that I myself am a loyal user of a website called Douban, the Chinese version of IMDb. I always look up the rating before deciding to watch a movie. So I believe data visualization like this project can somewhat help audiences like myself to choose more reliable movies.



Movies shown here are those whose votes are more than 1000.

The data are not up-to-date.
